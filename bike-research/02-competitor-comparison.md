## Detailed Competitors

### Canyon Endurace CF 7 — €2,049
- **Best value carbon bike in NL**
- Full carbon frame, 105 mechanical, 8.6 kg, 35mm clearance
- Same geometry as flagship CFR
- S15 VCLS 2.0 compliant seatpost for comfort
- Direct-to-consumer (no dealer margin)

### Canyon Endurace CF 7 AXS — €2,499
- SRAM Rival AXS **wireless electronic** shifting
- **Includes power meter** — unique at this price
- 8.5 kg, 35mm clearance
- Best overall spec for €2,500

### Canyon Endurace CF 7 Di2 — €2,999
- 105 Di2 electronic, **8.2 kg** (lightest in class)
- At the top of budget but exceptional value

### Giant Defy Advanced 2 — €2,699–€2,799
- Advanced-Grade carbon frame, 8.7 kg
- Shimano 105 mechanical, **40mm tyre clearance**
- D-Fuse vibration-damping seatpost and handlebar
- Tubeless-ready tyres included

### Trek Domane SL 5 Gen 4 — €2,999
- 500 Series OCLV Carbon with **IsoSpeed** decoupler
- Internal frame storage in down tube
- 38mm clearance, fender/rack mounts
- 9.0 kg — heaviest in carbon class

### Specialized Roubaix SL8 Sport — €2,999
- **Future Shock 3.1** (20mm suspension travel)
- FACT 10R carbon frame, **40mm clearance** (biggest)
- 9.07 kg — heavy but comfortable
- S-Works Mondo tyres

### Merida Scultura Endurance 4000 — €2,399
- CF3 carbon frame (same as higher models), 9.3 kg
- Carbon seatpost, Shimano 105 mechanical
- Integrated multitool under saddle

### Van Rysel EDR CF 105 — ~€2,499
- **Carbon wheels at €2,500** (VR35 Lite)
- High-modulus carbon frame, ~8.5 kg
- 38mm clearance, UCI-approved
- Decathlon's premium brand

### Bianchi Infinito 105 — €2,649–€2,699
- **Carbon wheels** (Velomann Palladium, 33mm)
- Full carbon frame, Shimano 105 mechanical
- Pirelli Cinturato Velo TLR 32mm tyres
- Italian heritage, ~9.0 kg

### Koga Roqa Prestige — €1,999 on Marktplaats (new)
- **Cheapest new carbon endurance bike available**
- **45mm tyre clearance** — class-leading, can run gravel tyres
- 105 2×12 mechanical, hydraulic disc, DT Swiss P1800 wheels
- Dutch brand, 24-month warranty
- 8.9 kg — heavier than the 7.9 kg quoted by some retailers
- Available from Fietscentrum Zuidlaren (maat L) and Fietsshop Kuijper (51 cm)

### Cannondale Synapse Carbon 5 — €2,699 on Marktplaats (new)
- Full carbon frame with SmartSense (integrated lights)
- Shimano 105 2×12 mechanical, hydraulic disc
- Classic endurance geometry — proven comfort
- ~35 mm clearance, ~8.7 kg
- Available from multiple Dutch dealers on Marktplaats

### BMC Roadmachine Three — ~€2,999
- Swiss brand, Tuned Compliance Concept
- Integrated cockpit, down tube storage
- Integrated rear light
- Shimano 105 mechanical, ~9.1 kg

### Sensa Giulia GF Tour — €1,999
- Dutch brand, full carbon frame
- Shimano 105 mechanical, ~9.2 kg
- Cheapest full-carbon + 105 option
- Sold via Mantel.com
