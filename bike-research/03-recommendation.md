# Recommendation — Best Road Bike in €2,000–€3,000 (NL)

## By Priority

### 1. Best Value: **Cube Attain C:62 SLX — €2,499**
- Carbon frame + **105 Di2 electronic shifting**
- 8.4 kg, carbon cockpit
- Available at local bike shops (Cube dealer network)
- Only downside: 34mm tyre limit

### 2. Best Spec: **Canyon Endurace CF 7 AXS — €2,499**
- Wireless SRAM Rival AXS + **power meter included**
- 8.5 kg, 35mm clearance, comfortable VCLS seatpost
- Direct order only (canyon.com/nl-nl)
- No local dealer support

### 3. Best Lightest: **Koga Roqa Prestige — ~€2,699**
- **7.9 kg** + **45mm tyre clearance**
- Dutch brand (great dealer support in NL)
- Can ride road to light gravel
- Shimano 105 mechanical

### 4. Best Comfort: **Specialized Roubaix SL8 Sport — €2,999**
- Future Shock 3.1 suspension, 40mm tyres
- Most comfortable over rough roads
- At the top of budget

### 5. Best All-Rounder: **Giant Defy Advanced 2 — €2,699**
- 40mm clearance, tubeless ready, 8.7 kg
- D-Fuse comfort tech, excellent frame
- Solid dealer network in NL

---

## Decision Flowchart

```
What's most important?
│
├─ Electronic shifting → **Cube Attain C:62 SLX** (€2,499, 105 Di2)
│  └─ Budget slightly higher? → Canyon Endurace CF 7 Di2 (€2,999, lighter)
│
├─ Pure value → **Canyon Endurace CF 7 AXS** (€2,499, wireless + power meter)
│  └─ Don't want direct order? → Cube Attain C:62 SLX
│
├─ Light weight + versatility → **Koga Roqa Prestige** (7.9 kg, 45mm tyres)
│
├─ Comfort on bad roads → **Specialized Roubaix SL8 Sport** (Future Shock)
│
├─ Big tyre clearance → **Giant Defy Advanced 2** (40mm) or Roubaix (40mm)
│
└─ Want carbon wheels? → **Van Rysel EDR CF 105** (~€2,499)
```

---

## If You Go With the Cube Attain

**Get the C:62 SLX** (not the C:62 Race). The €800 extra for 105 Di2 + carbon cockpit is worth it. You get:
- Electronic shifting (no cable stretch, perfect shifts always)
- Carbon handlebar and seatpost (better vibration damping)
- Better wheels (Newmen vs ACID)
- ~0.8 kg lighter

At €2,499 it sits perfectly in your budget.
