## Quick Reference: Minimum Requirements for Alpe d'Huez

| Requirement | What to look for | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **Gearing ≤1:1** | 50/34 + 11-34 (or 11-36) | 13% gradients need a climbing gear. 34/34 = 1.0 minimum. |
| **Disc brakes** | Hydraulic disc | Wet alpine descents. Rim brakes overheat. |
| **Tyre clearance ≥32mm** | 35-40mm ideal | Run 32mm tyres at lower pressure for comfort. |
| **Endurance geometry** | Stack/reach >1.45 | Upright position for 118km. Race geometry = back pain. |
| **Carbon frame** | Carbon (or quality alu) | Comfort over 8+ hours. Carbon absorbs road buzz. |
| **Clip-on bars** | Budget €100-200 | Aero position for flat sections saves energy for climbs. |
