## What to Watch For

- **Canyon specific:** The cockpit uses unique parts — check stem/bars aren't damaged
- **VCLS seatpost** is excellent but make sure the flip-head mechanism works
- **Gearing:** Ideally 50/34 + 11-34 (some come with 52/36 which is harder for climbing)
- **Tyre clearance:** 35mm is plenty for Alpe d'Huez

## Verdict

**Best used Di2 deal under €2,500.** The CF SL frame is light (8.0 kg), the VCLS seatpost is the most comfortable in class, and Ultegra Di2 is a step up from 105 Di2. If you can find one well-maintained at ~€2,200, it's a better bike than the Cube Attain C:62 SLX for ~€300 less.
